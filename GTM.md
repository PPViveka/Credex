## 🎯 The ICP: Exact Target User Persona

Our Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) is not a generic "startup founder." We are targeting the **SaaS Founding Engineer** or **Fractional CTO** at early-stage startups (Seed to Series A, team size 5 to 30, with 3 to 15 developers).

*   **Why them?** They manage the technology stack and developer license approvals. Unlike a non-technical CEO, they understand the technical tradeoffs (e.g., Claude API vs. Cursor Pro). Unlike an individual developer, they actually see the unified Stripe invoices, feel the burning irritation of paying $1,500/month for overlapping AI subscriptions, and are incentivized to optimize operational expenses.

## 📈 First 100 Users in 30 Days ($0 Budget Roadmap)

We will execute a 3-part tactical roadmap to acquire our first 100 validated audits without spending a single dollar on ads:

### 1. Reddit Case-Study Funnel (Days 1–10)
*   **The Play:** Write a highly detailed, data-backed post in `r/startups` and `r/ycombinator` titled: *"We audited 12 startup AI stacks and found an average of 32% billing waste. Here is exactly where the leaks are."*
*   **Content:** Breakdown real examples of dual Cursor/Copilot licenses, un-utilized seat minimums, and runaway API agents.
*   **Call-to-Action:** Provide a direct link at the end: *"We built a free tool to run this audit on your own stack in 45 seconds: [Link]"*.
*   **Target:** 40 users.

### 2. Manual "Micro-Audit" Cold Replies on X (Days 11–20)
*   **The Play:** Monitor keywords like "OpenAI bill," "Stripe SaaS spend," "Claude API cost" on X.
*   **Action:** When a founder shares a screenshot of their mounting SaaS spend, respond with a public, highly tailored micro-audit in their replies. E.g.: *"Hey @[Founder]! Noticed you have 4 devs on Claude Team. Anthropic bills a 5-seat minimum ($150/mo) for Team accounts. You'd save $1,080/year immediately by moving them to Claude Pro individual accounts ($80/mo total) since you aren't sharing workspaces. Built a free tool that runs 15+ of these checks: [Link]"*.
*   **Target:** 35 users.

### 3. Accelerator/VC Portfolio Outreach (Days 21–30)
*   **The Play:** Scraping LinkedIn for "Founding Engineer" or "VP of Engineering" at YC/Techstars startups currently in the active cohort.
*   **Outreach:** Send a direct, zero-fluff message offering to run a free audit. *"Hey [Name], noticed you're scaling the dev team at [Company]. We built a free 45-second tool that audits dev stack AI spend (redundant Cursor/Copilot seats, API limits) and benchmarks it against other YC teams. No login required: [Link]"*.
*   **Target:** 25 users.
